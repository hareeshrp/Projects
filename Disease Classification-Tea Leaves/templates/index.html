Tea Leaf Disease Classification:


The biggest challenge to tea growers is to produce tea without microbial or pesticide damage. Since leaves are the harvested product in tea, so leaf diseases play an important role. Lack of awareness and care to leaves causes unfavorable impacts on plants, product quality, and quantity get reduced. The symptoms of the disease can be observed on the leaves. The leaf shows symptoms by changing color or showing spots on it. The identification of these diseases is made manual, which can consume more time or may be costly. The idea is to identify and classify the diseases accurately from leaf images automatically.
